BFT and POW

In Bitcoin, The longest chain will be chosen as the mainchain. And we use pow to make sure that it will cost a lot to change the mainchain. So there is a thing called 51% computing power attack.

In Byteball, it uses witness to choose mainchain. If more than half of witnesses are down, attacked or even attacked, the whole chain will down. And due to many reasons, the amount of witnesses is only 13.

So we, Trustnote, decided to use another method to ensure the robust of mainchain. So we decided to combine POW and BFT. The witnesses will be elected by mining, and the first eight(which is consensused by all nodes) will be choosen. They will use BFT to choose how many units and which units is packed into the witness unit. This algorithm is called TrustMe.
